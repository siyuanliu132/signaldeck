      Decision cues

        Why it feels real

        Not a toy chatbot. A decision-support workflow.

        SignalDeck combines live market data, operator-driven screening, and explainable score logic so users can understand why a name surfaced, not just see a mysterious answer.

        Claim discipline

        No honest team should promise win rate without testing.

        This product is positioned as an opportunity-discovery and risk-triage system. Any future claim about hit rate would need backtesting, paper trading, and live validation against costs and slippage.

        Why it can become defensible

        Data, evaluation, and user workflow are the moat.

        The roadmap is clear: expand the universe, log historical signals, evaluate outcomes, measure precision by setup type, and learn which filters improve operator decisions.

        SignalDeck is built for opportunity discovery and risk filtering. It does not promise guaranteed returns, and any win-rate claim would require rigorous backtesting and live paper-trading evaluation.
